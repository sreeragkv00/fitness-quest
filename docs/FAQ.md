# FAQ - Fitness Quest

## General Questions

**Q: Is Fitness Quest really free?**
A: Yes! It's 100% free and open-source. No ads, no premium tiers, no paywalls.

**Q: Can I use it offline?**
A: The mobile app can work offline with limited functionality. Web requires internet.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. Your data is encrypted and stored securely in MongoDB.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Settings > Data > Export (downloads as JSON)

## Account & Profile

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Settings > Security > Change Password

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. Settings > Advanced > Delete Account (permanent)

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**
A: Yes, use different emails for each account.

**Q: How do I change my username?**
A: Settings > Profile > Edit > Update Username

## Workouts & Quests

**Q: How much XP do I get per workout?**
A: Depends on duration and intensity:
- Base: 50 XP
- Duration: 1 XP per 10 minutes
- Intensity multiplier: 1x (light), 1.5x (moderate), 2x (intense)

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: Your streak resets, but you can start a new one tomorrow!

**Q: Can I skip a quest?**
A: Yes. Each quest can be skipped once per day.

**Q: How many quests per day?**
A: 3 daily quests: Easy, Medium, Hard

**Q: Do I have to complete quests to workout?**
A: No! Quests are optional. You can log any workout anytime.

## Leveling & Achievements

**Q: How many levels are there?**
A: 100 total levels. Level 1 is the start.

**Q: How much XP per level?**
A: 1000 XP per level. 100,000 XP total to max level.

**Q: Can I lose levels?**
A: No. Levels only go up. You can never go backwards.

**Q: How do I unlock achievements?**
A: Complete specific requirements:
- Level milestones
- Streak goals
- Total workout counts
- Social activities

**Q: Can I lose achievements?**
A: No. Once unlocked, they're permanent.

## Guilds

**Q: What are guilds?**
A: Guilds are groups where members compete and encourage each other.

**Q: How do I create a guild?**
A: Click "Create Guild" and fill in the details.

**Q: Can I join multiple guilds?**
A: No. You can only be in one guild at a time.

**Q: What are guild benefits?**
A: 
- Shared leaderboard
- Guild-wide challenges
- Team achievements
- Member badges

**Q: Can I leave a guild?**
A: Yes, anytime. But if you're the leader, someone else becomes leader.

**Q: What's the guild level?**
A: Guilds have 10 levels based on total member XP.

## Nutrition Tracking

**Q: Is nutrition tracking required?**
A: No, it's completely optional.

**Q: Can I search foods?**
A: Yes, we have a database of common foods.

**Q: Can I log custom foods?**
A: Yes, enter calories and macros manually.

**Q: Can I set daily goals?**
A: Yes, in Settings > Nutrition > Daily Goals

## Technical Issues

**Q: App won't start**
A: Try:
1. Clear app cache
2. Restart your phone
3. Reinstall the app
4. Update to latest version

**Q: Workouts not saving**
A: Check:
1. Internet connection
2. App permissions
3. Storage space
4. Try logging out/in again

**Q: Not getting XP**
A: Ensure:
1. Workout was marked as "completed"
2. Duration is correct
3. No app errors (check logs)

**Q: Leaderboard shows wrong rank**
A: Rankings update every minute. Refresh the app.

## Platform-Specific

**Q: Can I use both web and mobile?**
A: Yes! Your data syncs across platforms.

**Q: Which is better, web or mobile?**
A: Both have the same features. Mobile is better for on-the-go logging.

**Q: Is there a desktop app?**
A: Not yet. Use the web app on desktop.

## Privacy & Security

**Q: What data do you collect?**
A: Only what you provide. No tracking, no ads, no third parties.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: MongoDB (secure cloud database)

**Q: Can you see my workouts?**
A: No. Only you can see your private data.

**Q: Is HTTPS enabled?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted in transit.

**Q: How long is data kept?**
A: Until you delete your account. Backups kept for 30 days.
