# User Guide - Fitness Quest

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
1. Download the app or visit the web app
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Fill in your profile information:
   - Name
   - Avatar (optional)
   - Height & Weight
   - Fitness level (beginner/intermediate/advanced)
5. Select your fitness preferences

### 2. Complete Your Profile
After signup, you'll be asked about:
- **Fitness Level**: Choose based on your experience
- **Workout Types**: Select exercises you enjoy
- **Equipment**: What you have available (gym/home/outdoor)
- **Time Availability**: Minutes per day you can commit

## Core Features

### 🎮 Leveling System

**How Leveling Works:**
- Start at **Level 1**
- Earn **XP** from completed workouts and quests
- Every 1000 XP = 1 level up
- Max level: 100

**XP Sources:**
- Complete workouts: 50-200 XP
- Daily quests: 100-300 XP
- Achievements: 50-500 XP
- Streaks: Bonus XP

### 🗺️ Daily Quests

Each day you get 3 quests:
1. **Beginner Quest** - Easy (30 min workout)
2. **Intermediate Quest** - Medium (45 min workout)
3. **Epic Quest** - Hard (60+ min workout)

**Completing Quests:**
- Start a quest
- Complete the workout
- Earn XP and rewards
- Build your streak

**Streaks:**
- Complete a quest every day
- Longer streaks = more XP bonus
- Lose streak if you miss a day

### 🏆 Achievements

Unlock badges by:
- Reaching level milestones
- Completing workout types
- Building streaks
- Hitting personal records
- Social activities

**Categories:**
- 🦾 Level Achievements
- 💪 Strength Achievements  
- ❤️ Cardio Achievements
- 🤝 Social Achievements

### 📊 Workouts

**Logging a Workout:**
1. Tap "Start Workout"
2. Choose workout type
3. Log exercises with sets/reps
4. Track duration
5. Get XP when complete

**Workout Types:**
- **Strength** - Weight training, bodyweight
- **Cardio** - Running, cycling, HIIT
- **Flexibility** - Yoga, stretching
- **Sports** - Basketball, soccer, etc.

### 👥 Guilds (Social)

**Join a Guild:**
1. Browse available guilds
2. Click "Join"
3. You're in!

**Create a Guild:**
1. Tap "Create Guild"
2. Add name, description, logo
3. Invite friends
4. Set guild rules

**Guild Features:**
- Guild leaderboard
- Weekly challenges
- Shared progress
- Guild chat
- Member achievements

### 🍎 Nutrition Tracking

**Log Food:**
1. Tap "Add Meal"
2. Search food database
3. Enter quantity
4. View macros

**Daily Summary:**
- Total calories
- Protein/Carbs/Fat breakdown
- Comparison to goals

### 📈 Analytics & Stats

**View Your Stats:**
- Total workouts
- Total minutes exercised
- Calories burned
- Streak information
- Weekly/Monthly trends

**Charts:**
- Workout frequency
- XP progression
- Calorie vs burn
- Muscle group breakdown

## FAQ

**Q: Can I change my fitness level?**
A: Yes! Go to Settings > Fitness Profile > Edit

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: Your streak resets, but you can start a new one tomorrow!

**Q: How do guilds help?**
A: Guilds provide accountability and friendly competition

**Q: Is nutrition tracking required?**
A: No, it's optional but helpful for holistic fitness

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes! Settings > Data > Export

## Troubleshooting

**App crashes on startup**
- Try uninstalling and reinstalling
- Clear app cache
- Check for updates

**Workouts not saving**
- Ensure you have internet connection
- Try logging out and back in
- Contact support if issue persists

**XP not awarded**
- Refresh the app
- Check quest requirements were met
- Contact support with workout details
